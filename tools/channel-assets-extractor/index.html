Best Practices for YouTube Banners

If you are downloading competitor banners for inspiration, notice the "Safe Zone" in the middle. Because YouTube banners are displayed differently on TVs, Desktop computers, and Mobile phones, top creators always place their main text, upload schedule, and logos right in the center.

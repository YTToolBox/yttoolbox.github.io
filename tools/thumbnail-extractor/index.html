Yes, thumbnails are public assets served by YouTube. However, remember that the intellectual property belongs to the original creator. Use these images for research, fair-use reaction videos, or inspiration. Avoid re-uploading someone else's thumbnail as your own to prevent copyright issues or "Reused Content" strikes.

How to Choose the Best YouTube Tags in 2026

One of the biggest mistakes new creators make is guessing their video tags. They type random words hoping the algorithm will pick them up. Short-Tail vs. Long-Tail Keywords

If you have a small channel, competing for a short-tail keyword like "iPhone" or "Gaming" is nearly impossible. Instead, you need to target Long-Tail Keywords. These are specific phrases like "iPhone 16 Pro Max camera test in low light". While the search volume is lower, the competition is virtually zero, allowing you to easily rank on Page 1. Our tool specializes in generating these long-tail variations.

How Our Deep-Context Engine Works

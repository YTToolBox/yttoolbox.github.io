Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to YT ToolBox ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our website (yttoolbox.github.io).

1. Information We Collect

YT ToolBox is designed to be a frictionless tool for creators. We do not require account creation, and we do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number to use our core extraction tools.

2. Use of YouTube API Services

Our tools utilize the official YouTube Data API to fetch public information like video tags, thumbnails, and channel data. By using YT ToolBox, you agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

3. Log Data & Cookies

Like most websites, we may collect non-identifiable log data (such as browser type, device type, and basic analytics) to improve our service. We also use third-party advertising partners (like Google AdSense) which may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your visit to this and other websites.

4. Data Storage

We do not store the video links you process or the data you extract. All processing happens dynamically via our secure Cloudflare API routing.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us via our Contact Page.
